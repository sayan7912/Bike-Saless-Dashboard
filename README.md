# 🚲 Bike Sales Dashboard | Tableau

An interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze customer purchasing behavior and identify key factors influencing bike purchases. The dashboard provides insights into demographics, regional sales, occupations, and income patterns through an intuitive and interactive interface.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to explore the Bike Sales dataset and answer business questions such as:

- Which region has the highest bike purchases?
- What is the average income and age of buyers?
- How do bike purchases vary by occupation?
- How does purchase behavior differ by gender?
- What relationship exists between buyer age, income, and bike purchases?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
  - Total Bike Purchases
  - Average Buyer Income
  - Average Buyer Age

- Interactive Visualizations
  - Bike Purchases by Region
  - Bike Purchases by Occupation
  - Purchase Percentage by Gender
  - Bike Purchases & Average Income by Age Group

- Interactive Filters
  - Gender Filter
  - Age Bin Slider

- Dashboard Actions
  - Selecting visuals filters the remaining charts dynamically.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- North America recorded the highest number of bike purchases.
- Professional and Skilled Manual occupations contributed the most purchases.
- The average buyer income is **$56,360**.
- The average buyer age is **44 years**.
- Bike purchases are highest among middle-aged customers before declining in older age groups.
